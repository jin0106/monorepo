8. Measures to Ensure the Security of Personal Information

The company takes the following protective measures to ensure the security of personal information in processing: Protection against loss, theft, leakage, alteration, and damage of personal information is ensured.

a. Technical Protection Measures

  • The company makes every effort to prevent the leakage or alteration of user's personal information due to intrusions into the information and communication network, such as hacking and computer viruses, by obtaining relevant security certifications.
  • The company employs up-to-date antivirus programs to prevent the leakage or damage of personal information or data by computer viruses.
  • For sensitive personal information, the company uses security measures (SSL) to securely transmit personal information over the network through encryption communication.
  • To guard against external intrusions like hacking, the company utilizes a web firewall system.

 

b. Administrative Protection Measures

  • The company operates a dedicated department for the protection of user's personal information and makes every effort to promptly address any issues through the establishment and management of a privacy policy and compliance.
  • Access to personal information is restricted to the minimum number of personnel with authorization, and the company emphasizes compliance with relevant laws, internal policies, and security commitments for personnel handling personal information through administrative measures, including training.
  • The transfer of responsibilities among personnel handling personal information is carried out thoroughly while maintaining security, and responsibilities for personal information incidents are clearly defined for employees both upon hiring and termination.
  • In the event of any loss, leakage, alteration, or damage to personal information resulting from errors by personnel, including personal information handlers, or technical management issues, the company will promptly inform users of the facts and take appropriate measures and compensation.

 

9. Information on the Installation/Operation and Refusal of Personal Information Automatic Collection Devices

The company uses "cookies" and "pixels" on its website to provide individualized custom services based on the analysis of user service usage behavior. Cookies are small pieces of information sent from the server used to run the website to the user's computer browser, and pixels are small pieces of code on the website to determine whether a user has interacted with specific content. Cookies may also be stored on users' PC hard drives.

During this process, the user's advertising ID (ADID, IDFA, etc.) and behavioral information may be automatically collected and used, but they are not combined with personally identifiable information. An advertising ID refers to an ID assigned by the mobile operating system (OS) for the purpose of delivering personalized ads, while behavioral information refers to user activity data on the internet that can help understand and analyze user interests, preferences, and habits, such as website visit history, app usage history, purchase history, and search history.

The company allows third parties, including advertising agencies, to collect the user's advertising ID and behavioral information through these cookies/pixels.

Users can refuse this at any time using web browsers or smartphone settings. Here's how to do it:

IE

Tools > Internet options > Privacy > Advanced > Block

Chrome

More > Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data > Block

Microsoft Edge

Tools > Internet options > Privacy

Safari

Settings > Privacy > Setting Cookies Block

 

The company uses web analytics tools like Google Analytics to analyze user service usage information, such as clicks and conversions.

If you wish to stop web analytics, you can refer to the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on Download Page to download and install the add-on that allows you to opt-out of Google Analytics tracking. This add-on is available for various web browsers and provides users with a way to prevent their data from being collected by Google Analytics when browsing websites that use this tracking service.
